Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the "Body Cam: Progress tracker" app ("we", "us", "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated - November 24, 2025

Information We Collect

Photos: The App allows you to take and store body-progress photos. All photos remain on your device only unless you manually export or share them.

App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage information (e.g., feature usage, crash logs) to improve the App. This data does not identify you.

How We Use Information

  • To provide core functionality (capturing and displaying your photos)
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To fix bugs and ensure stability

We do not use your photos for analytics, training, or third-party purposes.

Data Storage

All photos and personal data are stored locally on your device. We do not operate external servers for storing your photos.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your data with third parties. The only time data leaves your device is if you choose to export or share photos.

Third-Party Services

If we use third-party analytics or crash reporting tools, they receive only anonymous technical data. No photos or personal identifiers are shared.

Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for users aged 4+ and does not collect personal information from children.

Security

We use standard security practices to protect your data; however, full security cannot be guaranteed. Keeping your device secure is recommended.

Your Rights

You may delete your photos and app data at any time directly from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available on this page.
